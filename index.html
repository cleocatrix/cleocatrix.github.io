About me...

...or, rather, us :)

Hi and welcome to Cleocatrix! It's Helie (me), my muse and inspiration Cleo, and my most ardent supporter Michael. I'm a self-taught artist working in a variety of media, mostly watercolor and oil. The smallest feline is my favourite subject, but I also enjoy painting dogs, birds, landscapes, and plants.
